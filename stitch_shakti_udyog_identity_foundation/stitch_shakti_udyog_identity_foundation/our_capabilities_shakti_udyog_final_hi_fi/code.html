The Manufacturing Process

A seamless, vertically integrated workflow.

architecture
Phase 01

Enquiry & Drawing Review

Our engineering team analyzes 2D drawings and 3D CAD models to assess manufacturability. We conduct gating system design simulation and solidification analysis using MAGMASOFT to ensure defect-free castings before tooling begins.

precision_manufacturing
Phase 02

Pattern Development

In-house tool room equipped with VMC machines for precision pattern and core box manufacturing. We utilize aluminum, cast iron, and resin materials based on production volume requirements to ensure dimensional stability.

layers
Phase 03

Moulding & Core Making

High-pressure automated moulding lines ensure consistent mould hardness and accurate parting lines. Core shop utilizes cold box and shell core technologies to produce complex internal cavity structures.

local_fire_department
Phase 04

Melting & Pouring

Dual-track induction furnaces with strict temperature control. Every batch is analyzed via optical emission spectrometer before pouring. Specialized inoculation techniques guarantee uniform graphite distribution.

hardware
Phase 05

Fettling

Automated shot blasting and robotic grinding stations ensure a clean, consistent surface finish while removing gates and risers. This controlled process minimizes manual variation.

settings
Phase 06

Machining

Fully equipped CNC and VMC machine shop capable of delivering ready-to-assemble components. We maintain strict tolerances on complex geometries, turning raw castings into precision parts.

fact_check
Phase 07

Inspection & Dispatch

Rigorous quality assurance including CMM inspection, ultrasonic testing, magnetic particle inspection, and pressure testing. Castings are securely packaged to international standards.
